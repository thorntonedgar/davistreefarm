General Planning Notes

- Each booking is for a full-day event, includes venue rental, basic tables/chairs, parking, restroom access, event staff.
- Venue capacity: 200 guests per event.
- Per hour staffing costs include insurance, taxes, etc.
- All financials are preliminary and should be validated with local professionals.
- Get multiple quotes from local builders and vendors for accuracy.
- Consider contingency funds for unexpected costs.

High-Level Assumptions

- Average event size: 120 guests
- Average event duration: 8 hours
- Average staffing needs: 3-5 staff members per event
- Average decoration costs: $500 per event
