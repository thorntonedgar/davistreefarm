Staffing Model (Per Event)

- Event coordinator (1): Main client contact, manages event timeline, coordinates vendors, on-site for 12–14 hours. Cost: $70/hour, Total: $840
- Setup/cleanup crew (3): Handles setup and breakdown of tables, chairs, decor, trash removal, on-site for 6–8 hours each. Cost: $30/hour, Total: $336
- Parking/Security (1): Manages parking, assists guests, provides security. Cost: $18/hour, Total: $180

Note: Per hour costs include insurance, taxes, etc. Total staffing cost per event: $1,350

Optional: Additional staff for larger events or special requirements.
