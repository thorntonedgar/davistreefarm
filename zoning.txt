Conditional Use Permit: In agricultural or residential zones, operating a wedding venue typically requires a Conditional Use Permit (CUP). This permit allows for specific uses not ordinarily permitted in the zoning district, subject to approval by the Plan Commission and Board of Zoning Appeals. ​
